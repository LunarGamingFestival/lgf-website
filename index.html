FAQ

How do I participate in the Lunar Gaming Festival on Moonbeam?
Each event will have special requirements which you will find above, but the general rule is if you are staked with a participating collator at the snapshot date, and register afterwards on http://fidi.tech, you'll be eligible for the monthly giveaway.
Who's a participating collator?
Participating collators will always be listed above. Every event will have special rules, but follow the info here or contact a participating collator to stay involved. The Lunar Gaming Festival only rewards delegators of participating collators.
How do I participate in the Lunar Gaming Festival on Moonbeam?
If you are delegated to a Lunar Gaming Festival participating collator at the monthly snapshot, you will be eligible to claim your monthly NFT. So claim your NFT every month on AirLyft, and maybe there will be a surprise at the end of the festival depending on how many you have.

FAQ

What do FiDi and AirLyft have to do with this?
FiDi is a Moonbeam ecosystem partner that offers portfolio tracking and wallet-based analytics. For the festival, FiDi has a special festival page for registration of monthly giveaways which can be found on https://fidi.tech. AirLyft https://airlyft.one is an ecosystem partner that enables quests and fulfillment of NFT tokens amongst other things. You'll be able to claim your monthly NFTs via AirLyft.
Do I have to pay to play? How much could my winning be?
No payment or any other consideration is required, but only delegators of participating collators will be included. All rewards have no value but may be meaningful for in-game rewards.

FAQ

I delegate to these collators, but on Moonriver. Can I join the festival?
Of course, but know there are some caveats. Most of the festival games are launched on Moonbeam, and so all participation and rewards claiming will be on Moonbeam Network. But, this is a festival for everyone. If you are delegated with a participating collator on Moonbeam or Moonriver, you'll be added to the whitelist for each event. Just know that unless otherwise specified, any claims will only be made on Moonbeam Network. So Moonriver or Moonbeam -- stake with a participating collator and you are in.
