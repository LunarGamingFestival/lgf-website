GameFest FAQ

What was Moonie's LGF?
Moonie's LGF was a community gaming festival celebrating gamers and games built on Moonbeam Network. Every month, if you completed the tasks for each participating game, you would earn points that would make you eligible for prizes. It's ended now, but you can still join the announcements Telegram group to find out about new gaming initiatives. https://t.me/lunargamingfestival.com
